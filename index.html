Our Services

At Downs Accounting, we offer a comprehensive range of services to help our clients manage their finances and achieve their goals. Whether you're an individual, a small business, or a non-profit organization, our experienced team can provide the expert guidance and support you need.

Tax preparation

Our tax professionals can help you navigate the complex world of tax laws, ensuring that you pay the minimum amount of tax required by law. We can assist with filing your personal or business tax returns, as well as handling any audits or appeals.

Financial planning

Our financial planning services can help you develop a roadmap for achieving your financial goals, whether it's saving for retirement, buying a home, or starting a business. We can help you create a budget, invest your money wisely, and plan for the future.

Auditing

If you're a business owner, regular audits are an important part of maintaining the integrity and transparency of your financial records. Our auditors can provide independent assurance that your financial statements accurately reflect your financial position and performance.
